    WASTE NOT WANT NOT thrift store

    WNWN is downcity's new thrift store, art gallery, and textile workshop. 2nd Life is working with WNWN to supply artists with the materials they need to make the awesome upcycled clothing and artwork appearing in the store. You can visit the website for more info, or stop by any time Tuesday-Sunday, 10am-6pm.

    ACCEPTED ITEMS

    paper and cardboard products
    metal and wood scraps
    electronic parts and gadgets
    paints
    art supplies
    foam and fabric
    reasonably sized furniture
    costumes
    (save your regular clothing for salvation army, please)

    VOLUNTEER

    We are currently looking for more volunteers to open the trailer, sort, and post flyers. Send us an email if you're interested.

    HOW

    Bring your unwanted materials or a small monetary donation to exchange for stuff in the trailer. We have an assortment of art supplies, along with some household items. We don't accept clothes, but we do have scrap fabric.

    WHERE

    We're located at 31 Canal Street along the riverwalk between the Illustration Building and Design Center.
